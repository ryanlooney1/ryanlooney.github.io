About Ryan

I live near Boston with my family and spend my free time coaching youth sports, including field hockey, softball, baseball and basketball.

My approach

Curiosity → Partnerships → Impact

I bring genuine curiosity to problems, build real partnerships to solve them, and stay with it until the impact is real for billions of users and the business.

I recently earned a Master's in Anthropology (publishing a thesis on Santa Claus in North America), and I've learned that studying people is what I love to do in and out of work. It helps me listen, identify gaps and opportunities, and shape everything I do with the humans on the other side of the screen in mind.

I build alignment by showing up as myself and bringing people in from the start. I share everything openly, from the motivation to the data to the credit. I make sure people feel like partners.

At this stage of my career, my work is about leadership and scale as much as execution. Frameworks, AI integration, test-piloting new ways of working, and building systems that enhance team capabilities.

I'm excited about what AI will unlock for content pros, and I stay ahead of the shift by learning what works, creating agents to handle the initial research and prep, and delivering AI experiences that improve the user experience on Instagram and across Meta. We can do so much more with each technological leap.
