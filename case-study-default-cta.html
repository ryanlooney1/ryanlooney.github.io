Scaling ad CTA improvements across Meta

Home Next
Overview

I led content strategy to modernize Meta’s global ad CTA system, identify performance opportunities, and align stakeholders around a new default CTA.

Problem

The CTA system had grown without a clear ownership model or optimization plan. Many options had never been tested or analyzed, leaving performance on the table.

Approach

I developed a large-scale testing framework to evaluate CTA performance, partnered with product and research to align on testing priorities, and shared learnings across teams to build leadership consensus for optimized CTA strategies, including a new default.

Impact

This work established a scalable approach to CTA optimization and created a reusable framework to continually optimize CTAs by ad objective and vertical, enabling ongoing experimentation and monetization improvements.
