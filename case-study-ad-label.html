Overview

I led the cross-Meta initiative to change the "Sponsored" label to "Ad" across Meta apps to improve clarity, reduce visual clutter, and support monetization impact.

Problem

"Sponsored" was introduced in 2006 and was never systematically tested or optimized. This created an opportunity to simplify language while maintaining trust and aligning stakeholders across multiple apps.

Approach

I built working groups, partnered with cross-functional teams, and used AI tools to summarize decisions and risks. The result was a scalable plan that could roll out across Meta surfaces.
