Overview

I introduced system prompt writing as a content design capability, helping shape how AI-generated product content is created and evaluated.

section class="section">
Problem

Engineers owned prompt language end-to-end. Content design expertise wasn’t being applied at scale, which limited output quality and model performance.

Approach

I persuaded leadership, partnered with engineering and design, developed evaluation frameworks, created a playbook, and hosted hands-on learning sessions to help content designers apply system prompting practices.

Impact
